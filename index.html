Why now

Decision pressure is rising faster than decision intelligence.

☀️Climate risk is already operational
Food systems, infrastructure, water, health, fisheries, and regional resilience are already being reshaped by climate variability and long-term change.
🛡️Decision-makers must act under uncertainty
Capital allocation, adaptation planning, and policy settings are being locked in now, often with fragmented evidence and limited confidence calibration.
🔎Current tools often stop at pattern prediction
They can indicate what may happen, but are weaker at explaining why, how certain we are, and which actions remain robust across multiple futures.
Core need
Causal insight
Understand drivers, not only patterns.
Decision quality
Evidence-linked

Karrikins closes the gap between prediction and decision readiness.

  • Fragmented datasets and literature

🧠Karrikins
  • Causal attribution across heterogeneous data

  • Explicit uncertainty calibration
  • Action-oriented, evidence-linked outputs

  • Reusable platform across multiple sectors
